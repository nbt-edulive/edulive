a\) \(x + 1\)2 – y2
b\) x3 \+ 3x2 \+ 3x + 1
c\) 8x3 – 12x2 \+ 6x – 1
**Hướng dẫn giải:**
a\) \(x + 1\)2 – y2
= \(x + 1 – y\)\(x + 1 + y\)
b\) x3 \+ 3x2 \+ 3x + 1
= x3 \+ 3 . x2 . 1 + 3 . x . 12 \+ 13
= \(x + 1\)3
c\) 8x3 – 12x2 \+ 6x – 1
= \(2x\)3 – 3 . \(2x\)2 . 1 + 3 . 2x . 12 – 13
= \(2x – 1\)3
