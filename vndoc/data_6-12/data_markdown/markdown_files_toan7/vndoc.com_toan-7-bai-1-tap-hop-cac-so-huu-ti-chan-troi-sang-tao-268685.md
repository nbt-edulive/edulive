Ta có: ![0 = \\frac{0}{1}](https://i.vdoc.vn/data/image/blank.png)0=01
Vì ![0 \\in \\mathbb{Z};1 \\in \\mathbb{Z},1 \\ne 0](https://i.vdoc.vn/data/image/blank.png)0∈Z;1∈Z,1≠0 => ![0 \\in \\mathbb{Q}](https://i.vdoc.vn/data/image/blank.png)0∈Q
