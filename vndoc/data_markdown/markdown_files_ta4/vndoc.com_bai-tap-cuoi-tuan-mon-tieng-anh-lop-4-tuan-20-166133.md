**2\. Do the puzzle**
1\. DRIVER; 2. CLERK; 3. FARMER; 4. WORKER; 5. TEACHER; 6. DOCTOR
**3\. Read and match.**
1\. d; 2. e; 3. b; 4. a; 5. c
**4\. Put the words in the right order.**
1\. What does your father do?
2\. He is a teacher.
3\. Where does he work?
4\. He works in a primary school.
**5\. Write**
1 - She is a clerk.
3 - He is a farmer.
4 - She is a nurse/ She is a doctor.
