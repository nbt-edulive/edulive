I went by speedboat.
2\. I went to Cambodia last month.
How was it?
It was great.
How did you get there?
I went by minibus.
3\. Did you and your family go to Singapore?
Yes, we did.
How did you get there?
We went by plane.
4\. What did you do for your vacation?
I went to Nha Trang with my mom and dad.
How did you get there?
We went by helicopter.
