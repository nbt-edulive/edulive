Minh is \(6\) \_\_\_\_\_\_\_ his birthday party at home in the afternoon so I want to ask Hoa about what we shall buy and give him at the party. I also want to tell her to wait for me at home and I will go there to meet her \(7\) \_\_\_\_\_\_\_ foot and then go with her \(8\) \_\_\_\_\_\_\_ Minh’s house.

---|---|---|---  
2\. a. live| b. lives| c. to live| d. living  
3\. a. address| b. telephone| c. house| d. home

5\. a. in| b. at| c. to| d. on  
6\. a. have| b. to have| c. having| d. has  
7\. a. by| b. on| c. in| d. with  
8\. a. come| b. to| c. from| d. in
